Programs for Every Family

From Little Ninjas to leadership training, our curriculum keeps Siler City students active, focused, and confident all year long.

Little Ninjas (Ages 3-5)

Introduce early learners to Taekwondo through upbeat drills that reinforce balance, listening skills, and respect.

  • Play-based drills that teach kicks and stances
  • Life skills reinforced at every class opening and close
  • Positive mat etiquette that carries home and to school

Kids & Family Taekwondo

Train with Master Ara for heightened confidence, fitness, and perseverance using the World Taekwondo Federation curriculum.

  • Increased strength, stamina, and flexibility
  • Goal-setting that builds leadership on and off the mat
  • Family classes that keep siblings and parents progressing together

After School Success Program

Our community-driven after-school option blends academics, martial arts, and life skills for Chatham County families.

  • Daily martial arts classes and structured sports
  • A+ homework assistance and leadership coaching
  • Transportation from local schools and healthy routines

Hapkido & Self-Defense

Learn practical self-defense secrets that keep your family safe while elevating discipline and focus.

  • Joint-lock, escape, and situational awareness modules
  • Mind-body conditioning to reduce stress and sharpen reflexes
  • Advanced coaching for teens, adults, and leadership candidates
