Belt Lesson Guide

Before requesting a belt test, make sure the student has the required lessons for that belt and all four stripes.

Step 1: Track Completed Lessons

Count every completed class since the last belt promotion and compare it to the lesson minimum for the next belt goal.

Belt Lessons Required
White Belt25
High White Belt25
Yellow Belt25
High Yellow Belt25
Green Belt25
High Green Belt30
Blue Belt30
High Blue Belt32
Red Belt42
High Red Belt48

Step 2: Earn All Four Stripes

  1. Forms / Poomsae
  2. Self-defense
  3. Board breaking
  4. Sparring

When the student has the required lessons and all four stripes are signed off, they are ready to request a belt test in the portal.

Quick Example

  • Completed: 18 lessons
  • Current rank: Blue Belt
  • Required for Blue Belt: 30 lessons
  • Remaining: 12 lessons
