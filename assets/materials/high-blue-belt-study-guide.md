# High Blue Belt Study Guide

**Focus:** Leadership reps, spin kicks, and coaching cues for junior students.

## Key Skills
- Spin hook kick mechanics on target
- Counter-sparring drill with three linked combos
- Coaching cues for white/yellow belts

## Home Practice
- Design a 5-minute drill for lower belts and test it.
- Film spin hook kicks from two angles to check chamber height.
- Note feedback you give teammates after each class.
