Student Portal FAQ

How do I get my student ID?

New members receive their secure ID at sign-up. If you misplaced it, email aratkdsports@gmail.com and we will resend it within one business day.

Can families share one account?

Each student has a unique login so we can track rank progress accurately. Parents can store multiple IDs and login as needed.

What file types can I upload?

Upload a clear JPG or PNG photo of the official Certificate of Rank (up to 10MB). This helps the system verify authentic paperwork automatically.

What happens after I submit a belt test request?

Instructors review submissions weekly. Expect a confirmation email with prep tips and your scheduled test date.
